# Interactive Patient Journey Map

Map the end-to-end patient experience across Freddie (HIV/PrEP), Frida (ADHD), and Foria (Gender Care). Click any stage -- from Awareness through Follow-up -- to explore touchpoints, identify pain points, and surface AI improvement opportunities. Includes an emotional journey graph and group exercise prompts.

---

## Requirements

The lab brief describes a group exercise where each executive owns stages from their domain: Marketing owns Awareness & Search, Operations owns Lab Orders & Delivery, Product owns Self-Assessment & Follow-up. The tool should map journey stages, visualize emotional sentiment, and generate discussion prompts for cross-functional improvement.

![Interactive Patient Journey Map requirements](/img/labs/claude-desktop/interactive-patient-journey-map-requirements.png)

---

## Result

The artifact features brand selector pills (Freddie, Frida, Foria) and a horizontal stage map from Awareness through Pharmacy Delivery and Follow-up. Each stage is color-coded by health (Good, Needs Work, Pain Point). An emotional journey graph tracks patient sentiment across stages. Below, three columns surface Critical Pain Points, AI Improvement Opportunities, and Discussion Prompts.

## How to Recreate This

1. Define your patient journey stages and the touchpoints at each stage
2. Open [Claude](https://claude.ai) and start a new conversation
3. Describe the journey stages, known pain points, and ask for an interactive map with emotional scoring
4. Iterate -- add brand-specific variations, AI opportunity annotations, or group exercise facilitation prompts

:::tip
Patient journey maps are especially effective as group exercises where each leader owns stages from their domain. The emotional journey graph makes abstract "patient experience" conversations concrete and data-driven. See [Healthcare Prompt Scenarios](/prompting/healthcare-prompt-scenarios) for more healthcare-specific prompting patterns.
:::
