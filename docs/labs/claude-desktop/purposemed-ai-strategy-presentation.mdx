## How to Recreate This

1. Open [Claude](https://claude.ai) and start a new conversation
2. Describe your company context -- service lines, audience, and strategic priorities
3. Ask Claude to generate an executive briefing or presentation as an artifact
4. Iterate on sections -- adjust provider analysis, security frameworks, or recommendations

:::tip
Providing organizational context (service lines like Freddie, Frida, and Foria, target audience, strategic priorities) gives Claude the grounding it needs to produce relevant content rather than generic slides. See [Claude Projects](/context-and-memory/claude-projects) for how to persist this context across conversations.
:::
