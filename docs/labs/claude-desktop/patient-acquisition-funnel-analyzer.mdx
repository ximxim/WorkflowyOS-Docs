# Patient Acquisition Funnel Analyzer

Paste your marketing numbers for each funnel stage and this tool instantly calculates conversion rates, cost per acquisition, and identifies your worst drop-off -- across all three PurposeMed brands (Freddie, Frida, Foria) with a side-by-side comparison view.

---

## Requirements

The lab brief calls for a funnel analysis tool where you input ad spend and stage-by-stage numbers. The analyzer should calculate conversion rates between stages, highlight the worst drop-off point, compute cost per acquisition, and support per-brand filtering with a comparison mode.

![Patient Acquisition Funnel Analyzer requirements](/img/labs/claude-desktop/patient-acquisition-funnel-analyzer-requirements.png)

---

## Result

The artifact features brand tabs (Freddie, Frida, Foria, Compare All), an ad spend input, and a visual funnel with horizontal bars for each stage -- from Ad Impression through Click, Landing Page, and Assessment Started. Red warning badges flag the worst drop-off point with conversion percentage and cost-per-unit metrics.

## How to Recreate This

1. Export your marketing funnel data -- ad spend, impressions, clicks, landing page visits, assessments started
2. Open [Claude](https://claude.ai) and start a new conversation
3. Share your stage-by-stage numbers and ask for a funnel analyzer that highlights drop-offs and calculates CPA
4. Iterate -- add brand-level filtering, benchmark comparisons, or recommendation engines

:::tip
Multi-brand comparison is powerful for organizations with multiple service lines. Structuring your prompt around the specific metrics you track makes the output immediately actionable. See [Healthcare Prompt Scenarios](/prompting/healthcare-prompt-scenarios) for more domain-specific prompting patterns.
:::
